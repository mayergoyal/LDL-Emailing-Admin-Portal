In JIIT

LDL was introduced in JIIT in the late 20s after 2010

LDL Mission

To identify and educate children and make them self-dependent so that they can become dignified members of the society & have a common platform with others in terms of social equality and justice. To make children discover their hidden potentials so that they can be the change in society.

LDL Motive

Our motto is “Educate Empower Enlighten”. The members of the organization working together, providing the best fit education to these children. The seed of education will enable these children not just to grow but also bloom into responsible citizens of our country. This will empower them to stand on their own pillars in the society, to achieve success in life and rise as an enlightening star.

Kaushambi-Camp

Jiit runs two camps , kaushambi is one of them , this site is located in Gaziabad near Kaushambi Metro Station .Students here ranges from primary to secondary level (nursery to 12th) . The strength of children varies but lies under 15 . Students here are wise and active for their study part , many are even study in schools out of some are being sponsored by Team LDL JIIT
