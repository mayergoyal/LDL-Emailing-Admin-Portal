In JIIT

LDL was introduced in JIIT in the late 20s after 2010 . And now LDL JIIT is successfully running its two camps in Fortis and Kaushambi with dedicated core team of third year students along with active volunteers.

LDL Mission

To identify and educate children and make them self-dependent so that they can become dignified members of the society & have a common platform with others in terms of social equality and justice. To make children discover their hidden potentials so that they can be the change in society.

LDL Motive

Our motto is “Educate Empower Enlighten”. The members of the organization working together, providing the best fit education to these children. The seed of education will enable these children not just to grow but also bloom into responsible citizens of our country. This will empower them to stand on their own pillars in the society, to achieve success in life and rise as an enlightening star.

Kaushambi-Camp

Jiit runs two camps , kaushambi is one of them , this site is located in Gaziabad near Kaushambi Metro Station .Students here ranges from primary to secondary level (nursery to 12th) . The strength of children varies but lies under 15 . Students here are wise and active for their study part , many are even study in schools out of some are being sponsored by Team LDL JIIT

Fortis-Camp

Jiit runs two camps , Fortis is one of them , this site is located in Noida near Fortis Hospital .Students here ranges from primary to secondary level (nursery to 8th) . The strength of children varies but lies under 20 . Students here are wise and active for their study part , many are even study in schools out of some are being sponsored by Team LDL JIIT.

She For She Campaign

we are looking after Menstrual Hygiene of our children falling under the age group & also of their family members. While having discussion with few girls & visualising the problems they are facing, we thought of exploring possibilities in to it.

Cloth Distribution

We distribute Clothes to the needy ; the kids and their families . On Dewali , holi , Id , Independence day , Republic day , we ensure to provide clothes and basic stuff to them which they enjoy and accept happily.
